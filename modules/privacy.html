Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information this website collects and how it is used.

1 Information Collection

This website does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, sign-ups, forms, or data submissions of any kind.

The only data stored is your course progress and preferences, which are saved using your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Exists only on your device, in your browser
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never shared with any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

2 Cookies

This website does not use cookies. All local storage is handled through the browser's localStorage API, which is not a cookie.

3 Third-Party Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. While the site itself does not collect data, GitHub may collect basic technical information (such as IP addresses) as part of their hosting service. For details, please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

4 Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you have full control over it. To delete your data:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage for this site
  • Use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature
  • Use an incognito/private browsing window (no data is persisted)

The owner of this site is not responsible for any loss of locally stored data.

5 Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
